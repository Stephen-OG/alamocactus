About Alamo Cactos

Compassionate care for a healthier mind and life.

Our Mission

At Alamo Cactos Healing Stream Mental Wellness, we believe that mental health is a vital part of overall wellness. Founded on the principles of empathy, clinical excellence, and holistic support, our mission is to walk alongside individuals and families through their healing journey.

What We Do

We provide a range of evidence-based mental health services including psychiatric evaluations, therapy, medication management, and lifestyle support. Our team is made up of licensed professionals who deeply care about helping you thrive emotionally, mentally, and spiritually.

Meet Our Founder

Our founder, Opanuga O. Michael PMHNP-BC, established Alamo Cactos with a passion for healing and a heart for community. With over 15 years of clinical experience in mental health and wellness, she brings deep expertise and a warm, personal approach to care.

Her vision is to create a sanctuary for healing, where every person is seen, heard, and supported.

Our Core Values
